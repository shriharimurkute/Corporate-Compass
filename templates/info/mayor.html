About the Mayor / Chairperson

Information regarding the role and office of the Mayor (or Chairperson, depending on the type of municipality).

Role of the Mayor/Chairperson

The Mayor is the political head of the Municipal Corporation/Council. Their primary roles often include:

  • Presiding over the meetings of the elected Council.
  • Acting as the ceremonial head of the city for official functions.
  • Representing the city in various forums.
  • Providing political leadership and direction to the Council.
  • Working with the Municipal Commissioner/Chief Officer to ensure smooth functioning of the administration.
  • Acting as a bridge between the citizens and the municipal administration.

The extent of the Mayor's executive powers can vary significantly depending on the specific state laws governing the municipality.
